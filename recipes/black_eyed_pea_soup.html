Black-Eyed Pea Soup

Delicious and wholesome meal that will surely bring the family around the table

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dried black-eyed peas
  • 5 slices thick-cut bacon
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 large diced onion
  • 2 large diced carrots
  • 2 ribs diced celery
  • 1 and a half cups frozen chopped collard greens, thawed
  • 3 cloves of minced garlic
  • 3 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 smoked ham hock
  • half a cup of rice blend
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
